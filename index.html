Valerie Nelson is a 21-year-old artist who has been illustrating since she was a young child. She is currently studying at the Rhode Island School of Design and is using the profit from her pet portraits to help fund her studies.
Each pet portrait is painted in watercolour on an 8" x 8" piece of textured white watercolour paper. If you have any specific size, medium, etc. that you would prefer instead, let me know and we can work out your perfect pet portrait. The cost of one 8" x 8" watercolor portrait is $50.00 (plus approx. $5 for shipping). If you choose to have more than one pet in a painting, the cost is $50 per pet ($100.00 for a double portrait).

When requesting a pet portrait, please include a photo of the pet(s) you would like painted attached to the email.
